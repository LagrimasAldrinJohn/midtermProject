Yoshitsune, the Blademaster

Melee - Carry - Nuker - Disabler

Born in the time of war and upheaval, the young Yoshitsune saw his family killed in a rebellion while he survived under the care of a warlord. Studying the art of sword, he swore fealty to his lord, leading countless battles as he claimed enemy generals' head as trophies. As the master of the blades, his swords seem to dance around him, striking his foes at his will, while the warrior remains unstoppable. Breaking his sword is not enough to stop him, as he just simply takes his victims' as his own. The only swordsman who can use the ultimate move, Hassou Tobi, Yoshitsune proves that he is the sole Blademaster, as he dashes through the battlefield, leaving only headless corpses on his path.

Maelrawn, the Tentacular

Melee - Disabler - Nuker - Durable

How long has it been since the world cowered at the tendrils of Maelrawn, the Tentacular? Not many survived the demon's wrath, once he was summoned to wreck havoc. It has been so long since Maelrawn was able to roam freely that the world almost seems brand new to Maelrawn and that makes his corruption of its residents all the sweeter. In time, all will come to fear and love him, for it is better to love Maelrawn's terrible power, than to be his enemy.

Ainur, Warden of the Grove

Ranged - Disabler - Nuker

Ainur is a defensive support that can quickly turn a fight around with his abilities. Enemies must be wary if they choose to engage Ainur, as Light the Way and Black Swamp, can quickly put a stop to any initiation. Additionally, Protective Nature allows Ainur and any hero laning with him an increased chance to win the lane, as well as has its use during teamfights. Finally, The Great Grove is your big teamfight spell that is capable of turning a fight in more ways than one. The ability to provide health and mana to your allies is a great boon, but perhaps the best part of the ultimate is its ability to reduce the respawn timers of Ainar and his allies.

Fūjin, the Wind Spirit

Melee - Ganker - Nuker - Initiator

Fujin, one of the eldest of the spirits, and comrade of the Storm Spirit, controls the element of Air. He rarely takes direct action, instead preferring to manipulate things from afar. However, every few centuries or so, Fujin feels a need to demonstrate his power and remind the other spirits of the limits of their power. In an instant Fujin can strike his foes by using his power over the winds to strike at those that displease him. It doesn't take much to incur his wrath, at his old age Fūjin has become quite short tempered, leading to many a great windstorms around the world.
